# Blackboard Login (UTMB)

## Prereqs
- Browser: Chrome (signed into your profile).
- Username: `frtucker`.
- You will type the password/2FA; not stored in this playbook.

## Steps
1) Open `https://utmb.blackboard.com/`.
2) You are redirected to UTMB Web Login Service (`idp.utmb.edu`).
3) In the login form:
   - Click the `Username` field and enter your UTMB username (`frtucker`).
   - Click the `Password` field and enter your password (you type this).
   - Click the red `Login` button.
4) After authentication you land on Blackboard `Courses` page (sidebar shows Institution Page, Courses, Organizations, Calendar, etc.).
5) If prompted again later, repeat steps 1-3; session typically lasts ~12 hours.

## Notes
- If a Duo/2FA prompt appears, approve it to proceed.
- Use the course search bar or left sidebar to open specific courses once on the Courses page.
